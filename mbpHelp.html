Help Page

Welcome to « To Do List » site. For using the site, first you must to click on « Make a To DO » then when you go to there you can see todo's boxes. And you can see some buttons up the todo boxes.

« Save » button can save your todo list if you want and you can chose your todo list name for save.

« New » button can make a new todo list for you. Actually this button clear your todo list and save your todo list in history.

« Change Background » button change your background color.

With « Upload Background » button you can upload a photo from your gallery for your background.

With « Change Text Color » button you can change your color text.

And when you go out from the Make To Do page you can see the « History » button.

In « History » button you can see all of your todo listes.
